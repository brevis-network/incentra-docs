## Supported Chains

#### Data Source Chain

You can incentivize protocols deployed on the following chains:

* Ethereum
* BNB Chain

#### Reward Claim Chain

You can create campaigns with the reward tokens deployed on the following chains:&#x20;

* Ethereum
* BNB Chain

**NOTE**:  Reward claim and the protocol to be incentivized can be deployed on different chains. [Here](../developer-sdk/read-and-claim-rewards.md) are more details.
